Our Purpose

Our desire is to serve our community well in Christ’s name for His glory among all people. Spring Serve is committed to joining college students, campus ministries, and churches to further the Great Commission.

Christ commanded His followers to share His great news with those around them. One of the best ways to do this is through loving service. Christ came primarily to save, but we also see Him beautifully serving others throughout His life. There are many opportunities and capacities in which we can serve. God has allowed many people to find their home in Bowling Green and we should be faithful to bring them the good news and hope that we have.

How much does Spring Serve cost?

Spring Serve is free!

Can adults (non-students) volunteer?

Yes, we need adult volunteers! Be sure to register under your local Church’s name or your affiliation. Please also clarify that you are not a student.

Can anyone come to Spring Serve?

The evening worship services are open to anyone. The Saturday of serving is for registered participates only.

When is the application due?

The application is due April 2nd.

Where will SpringServe be?

Spring Serve will be hosted at Living Hope Baptist Church. Although, we will be serving in sevearl locations around Bowling Green.

Click here for directions.

Why should I be a part of Spring Serve?

This is an opportunity to bridge WKU students with the community where are living. If a student is looking for a church home, this is a great opportunity to serve alongside other churches. This also allows students to connect with campus ministries, and meet other brothers and sisters. Bowling Green is a city that is growing fast and there are many people who need to experience the love of Christ!
